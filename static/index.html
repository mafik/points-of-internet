Points of Internet

They can be integrated into any website.
Their purpose is to reward heroes & punish trolls.
Think of them as online version of Santa's naughty or nice list.

Everybody starts at 0 and can use points to improve or decrease rating of others up to credit limit of -1000.
Every 50 days there is half as much of them as before. If anyone is below 0, he gains points.

Embed & Extend

Points of Internet are open source! You can embed widget on your website... :

<iframe frameborder=0 width=350 height=40
  src="https://pointsof.net/iframe/#[email]"></iframe>

... use JavaScript:

<script src=https://pointsof.net/api.js></script>
<script>
  PI.get_balance("[email]", function(err, balance) {
    console.log(balance);
  });
</script>

... or simply a link:

<a href="https://pointsof.net/#[email]:5">
  Click here to send me 5 PI.</a>

Also, check out pointsof.net/db/ for daily database snapshots.

Who am I & why I'm doing this?

I'm Marek Rogalski and I think that a decent reputation system is the best way to combat paid comments & reviews on the internet. It could let honest people stay anonymous while retaining trust of others. Full transparency should make it resilient to malicious hacking and provide solid base for others to build upon.

Only known way to provide such reputation system in a distributed (and potentially hostile) environment such as the internet is through public & trusted database. There was no such thing, so I built one. I'm devoting a lot of my free time to maintain it (including resources of my home server) so if you can, please help me by donating some money:
